# 🌐 LAN, WAN and Internet

## 📖 Introduction

This document explains the differences between a **LAN**, a **WAN**, and the **Internet** in a simple way.

---

# 🖧 LAN (Local Area Network)

A **LAN** is a local network limited to a small geographic area such as:

- Home
- School
- Office
- Computer lab

## ✅ Characteristics

- Fast network
- Local connection
- Commonly uses:
  - Ethernet
  - Wi-Fi

## 🏠 Example

At home:

- PC
- Smartphone
- Game console
- Printer

All connected to the same Internet router → this forms a **LAN**.

---

# 🌍 WAN (Wide Area Network)

A **WAN** is a large network that connects multiple LANs over long distances.

## ✅ Characteristics

- Covers multiple cities or countries
- Uses:
  - Fiber optics
  - Telecommunication networks
  - Satellites

## 🏢 Example

A company with offices in:

- Paris
- London
- Tokyo

All offices connected together → this forms a **WAN**.

---

# 🌐 Internet

The Internet is the largest global network in the world.

It connects:

- Billions of devices
- Millions of LANs and WANs

## ✅ How it works

When you visit a website:

1. Your device connects through your LAN
2. Then through your Internet provider’s network (WAN)
3. Finally to the Internet

## 🌍 Examples of Internet services

- Google
- YouTube
- Wikipedia

---

# 📊 Summary

| Type | Size | Example |
|------|------|----------|
| LAN | Small local network | Home, school |
| WAN | Large extended network | International company |
| Internet | Global network | The Web |

---

# 🚗 Simple Analogy

- **LAN** → Roads inside your neighborhood
- **WAN** → Highways between cities
- **Internet** → The worldwide road system

---

# ✅ Conclusion

- A **LAN** connects nearby devices.
- A **WAN** connects multiple LANs over long distances.
- The **Internet** connects the entire world.
